Welcome to PIG DICE!

Hello! Welcome to a game of Pig Dice.
The aim is to score 100 points before your competitor does.
This game is played with a single six-sided die.
Keep on rolling the die until you are happy with the number of points you have scored, and hold your score to accumulate points.
The first player to reach a higher score closer to 100 wins!
Please note, however, that if you roll "1", you will lose all the points you have accumulated during your turn, and you lose your turn.
